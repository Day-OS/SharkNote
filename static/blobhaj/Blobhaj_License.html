Hey, Folks Might Read This. How About a Trans PSA?

Blobhaj and Blahaj support trans rights! If you're trans or trying to figure stuff out, the sharks suggest seeking out any local resources. We live in extremely online times and it can be easy to settle for an internet community. As valuable as that is, interacting in person and meeting trans folks of all ages does a LOT for your wellbeing. Heatherhorns is transgender, and always happy to talk, if you need somebody. Same address - electronicmail@heatherhorns.com. (I'm not a professional, and shouldn't replace any. I just realize that sometimes you gotta talk to *somebody*)
